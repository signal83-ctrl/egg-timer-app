Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 22, 2026

Welcome to Egg Timer. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

Egg Timer does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to use the core functionality of the app. We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or other personally identifiable information.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use Egg Timer, certain information may be automatically collected, including:

  • Device information (device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers)
  • App usage data (features used, session duration, interaction patterns)
  • Technical data (IP address, crash reports, performance diagnostics)
  • Advertising identifiers (Google Advertising ID) for ad personalization

2. Use of Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Egg Timer application
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
  • Display personalized advertisements through Google AdMob
  • Diagnose and fix technical issues

3. Third-Party Advertising - Google AdMob

Egg Timer is ad-supported and uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data to provide personalized ads. The information collected by AdMob includes:

  • Advertising identifiers (such as Google Advertising ID)
  • Device information and location data
  • Interaction with advertisements
  • IP address and user activity across apps and websites

Google AdMob is a service provided by Google LLC. For more information on how Google processes your data, please review:

  • Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google AdMob Partners: https://support.google.com/admob/answer/9012903

3.1 Opt-Out of Personalized Advertising

You can opt out of personalized advertising by:

  • Adjusting your device settings (Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization on Android)
  • Visiting the Google Ads Settings page: https://www.google.com/settings/ads
  • Using the Digital Advertising Alliance opt-out tool: http://www.aboutads.info/choices/
